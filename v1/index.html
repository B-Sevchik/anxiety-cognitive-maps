Thank you for participating in this study, which investigates how people are able to learn associations between stimuli. In this task, you were presented with a series of images that belonged to one of two clusters. We were interested in seeing if people are able to learn this structure, and how individual levels of anxiety related to learning the location of threatening versus neutral images faster and more accurately.
